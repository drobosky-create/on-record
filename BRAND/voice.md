# Brand Voice

## Principles
- Direct. Declarative. No hedging.
- Administrative, not motivational. The tool states standards; it does not cheer.
- Restraint over embellishment. Every added element must justify itself.
- Permanence in language: commitments are recorded, not suggested.

## Do
- Use plain, exact language.
- Speak in standards and non-negotiables.
- Let the object and the system do the persuading.

## Don't
- No decorative or "delightful" copy.
- No exclamation-driven encouragement.
- No softening qualifiers around commitments.
