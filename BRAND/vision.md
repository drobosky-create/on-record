# Vision

**Man on Record** exists on a single principle:

> Permanence changes behavior.

When a commitment is written into something that cannot be erased, refilled, or quietly abandoned, the person who made it behaves differently. The Record is the first physical instrument of that principle.

## What we are building

Not a planner. Not a journal. Not a productivity product.

An **operating system for accountability** — beginning as a printed object and expanding into digital tools, programs, and systems that apply the same principle to individuals and organizations.

The Record is simply the first manifestation.

## Where the principle can go

The principle is not confined to paper. Over time it can extend to:

- Executive coaching
- Organizational operating systems
- Leadership programs (including military and sales leadership)
- Couples and parenting
- Digital and enterprise accountability software

That is a platform, not a planner. This repository is structured to hold the platform, not just the first product.
