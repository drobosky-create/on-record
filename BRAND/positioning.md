# Positioning

## Category
The Record is positioned against publishers' short-run bookbinding standards, **not** against notebooks, planners, or print-on-demand products. The object must read as *administrative and serious*, never decorative.

## Anti-positioning (what we are not)
- Not a notebook brand
- Not a planner or journal brand
- Not a print-on-demand / creator product (Printify, Lulu, Amazon KDP)

Each of those fails on binding discipline, paper discipline, or consistency.

## The object carries the brand
The physical artifact carries the entire brand's credibility. If the object does not immediately communicate seriousness when a person opens it and lays it flat, the positioning has failed. This is a credibility investment, not a margin exercise.

## Emotional register
Permanence, restraint, seriousness, administrative weight. **Delight is poison here.** The design should encourage restraint, repetition, and systematization — not variation or visual interest.
