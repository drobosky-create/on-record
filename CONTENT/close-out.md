# Close-Out (in-book page)

The final page of the individual editions. It follows Day 90 and formally closes the record. Shared across Man on Record and Woman on Record — identical. In a **paired set**, the sealed reveal signature is bound *after* this page.

---

## Printed copy

**CLOSE-OUT — DAY 90**

Ninety days are counted. This is the accounting. Answer plainly; this record keeps no account but the true one.

Days kept: ______ / 90

The standard held:   YES ▢    NO ▢
_(one line — the truth of it)_
_____________________________________________

What changed in you:
_____________________________________________

_____________________________________________

This record is now closed. It cannot be reopened, erased, or restarted.

Signed ______________________   ____ / ____ / ______

---

## Layout notes
- Final recto page. Ruled lines for the written fields.
- YES / NO is the one permitted marked field here — consistent with the Daily Page's KEPT/BROKEN exception. Binary, no scale.
- No page number.
- In a paired set, the sealed reveal signature follows this page (see the-common-record/reveal-spec.md).
