# Weekly Escalation Page — Master

Appears once every seven Daily Pages. Built as an InDesign master. Where the Daily Page records, the Weekly Page reckons — it counts the week and raises the stakes for the next.

---

## Printed fields (in order)

**WEEK ________**            Days ______ to ______

**Days kept:** ______ / 7

**The pattern** _(one line — where you held, where you broke):_
_____________________________________________

**The escalation** _(what you raise next week — a standard made harder, or a new one added):_
_____________________________________________

**Signed** ______________

---

## Rules
- Master-page driven; identical every week.
- "Days kept" is a count the holder transfers from the week's Daily Pages — the book does not compute it for them.
- Escalation is required, not optional: the premise is that a standard met becomes the floor, not the ceiling.
- No decorative elements. No page number beyond the Week count.
