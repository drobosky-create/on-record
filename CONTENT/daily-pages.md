# Daily Page — Master

The repeating page, built as an InDesign master so every instance is identical (×90 for the 90-day edition). The Day count is the only numbering. Deliberately austere: it asks whether the standard held and what was done — nothing more.

---

**Signed** ______________   _(close the day — no day is left unsigned)_

---

## Rules
- The daily page is **locked**. Designers must not add fields, prompts, ornament, or ruling beyond what is specified.
- KEPT / BROKEN is the **one permitted marked field** — two small boxes, binary, no scale, no partial credit. **Any single break makes the day BROKEN.**
- One obligation. The form physically refuses a list.
- The record block is four lines.
- Every day is signed. The signature line is the point of the page.
- Day count is the only numbering; no folio/page numbers.
