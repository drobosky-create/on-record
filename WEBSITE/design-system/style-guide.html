Design System

The web and marketing brand system for On Record. Austere and administrative by intent — the same register as the object. No shadows, no rounded corners, no gradients except the silver-foil wordmark. Restraint over embellishment.

Oxblood is the primary accent (CTAs, links). Ink is text and dark surfaces. Bone is the paper. Maroon is hover/deep accent. The three cover colours — Ink (Man), Oxblood (Woman), Maroon (Common) — double as the brand palette.

03 · Buttons

Sharp corners, uppercase, tracked. Primary = oxblood; hover deepens to maroon. Ghost = ink outline. No shadows, ever.

04 · Form fields — ruled, like the book

Inputs are a single hairline underline — the same ruled write-in line used inside the book. Focus turns the rule oxblood. No boxes, no fills.

06 · Wordmark

On dark surfaces the wordmark carries a silver-foil treatment (the only permitted gradient). On light it is solid ink. Wordmark only — no tagline lockup.

07 · Principles

Do

  • Sharp corners, hairline rules, flat fills
  • Generous whitespace; let it breathe
  • Uppercase mono for labels & counters
  • Oxblood only where action matters

Don’t

  • No drop shadows or rounded corners
  • No gradients (except the foil wordmark)
  • No stock imagery or icons-as-decoration
  • No exclamation-driven, “delightful” copy
