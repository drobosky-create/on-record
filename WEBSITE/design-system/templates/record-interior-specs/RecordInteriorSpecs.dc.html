INTERIOR SPEC — THE COMMON RECORD (CO-AUTHORED · 45-DAY · SHIPS SEALED)
56 pp · Co-Initiation (three acts) → Joint Standards → Daily ×45 → Weekly after every 7th (×6) → Close-Out · two signatures on every closing page — a page closed with one signature is not closed
WHAT DIFFERS FROM THE INDIVIDUAL BOOKS
SHARED
Same trim, paper, binding, typeface, margins, rules, severity. Editions never drift.
EXTENT
45 days, not 90. Co-Initiation replaces the solo Initiation.
SIGNATURES
Two signature lines on every acknowledgment, declaration, daily, weekly, and close page. The dual signature deliberately rhymes with a vow.
UNLOCK
Ships sealed in the paired set. Opens only after both partners finish their 90 days and break the sealed reveal in their individual volumes. If only one finishes, this book never unlocks.
CONSENT
The exchange is voluntary and mutual at the moment of reveal. Never forced — packaging notes some couples should do it with a counselor present.
