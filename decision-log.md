# Decision Log

A permanent record of the decisions behind The Record. Newest first.

## v0.1 — Concept

- **Repository named `man-on-record`.** Named after the platform/company, not the book, so it can hold The Record plus future tools. Structured as a single monorepo for now; may split into `record-system` / `record-app` / `record-company` later.
- **Design tool: Adobe InDesign only.** Enforces print discipline. Canva, Figma, Google Docs, and Word are rejected. See [`BUSINESS/design-environment.md`](BUSINESS/design-environment.md).
- **Cover: matte black/charcoal with embossed silver title.** No gloss, no imagery.
- **Binding: Smyth-sewn / thread-sewn, lay-flat, non-refillable.** No spiral, disc, or glued-only. See [`PRINT/print-spec.md`](PRINT/print-spec.md).
- **Paper: 100–120 GSM uncoated, off-white/bone.** No pre-printed dot grid or ruling.
- **Vendor type: short-run bookbinder that serves publishers.** Not a notebook/planner brand or POD service. Hemlock under evaluation.
- **Initial run: 12 units.** Expect ~$40–$80/unit — a credibility investment, not a margin exercise.
- **Positioning: administrative and serious; delight is poison.**
