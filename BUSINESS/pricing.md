# Pricing

## Cost basis
- **Hand-bound proofs (2–3):** premium per-unit; expect roughly $120–300/unit for true hand binding.
- **~50-unit seed run:** budget realistically for a cloth case, silver foil, and lay-flat binding. Per-unit falls well below hand-binding but stays a premium tier — this is a credibility investment, not a margin exercise.
- A $10–20/unit quote signals the wrong quality tier.

## Retail
_To be set. Must reflect the object's positioning as a serious, permanent instrument — not a commodity notebook. Price against the 90-day edition as a completable, keepable artifact._
