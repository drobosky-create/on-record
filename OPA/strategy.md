# OPA — Strategy

_OPA = the go-to-market approach for The Record._

## Principle
The product sells on seriousness and permanence, not features. Distribution should reach people who already value accountability instruments: leaders, operators, and disciplined practitioners.

## Approach (to develop)
- Seed the object with a small set of credible early holders.
- Let the physical artifact do the persuading.
- Avoid mass, decorative, or discount-driven channels that contradict positioning.

> _Flesh out objectives, channels, and metrics as the launch plan firms up._
