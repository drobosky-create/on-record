# Marketing Plan

_Governs go-to-market from Prototype (`v0.5`) through Preorder (`v1.x`). Budget ceiling: **$5,000** through preorder. Working retail band: **$95–$125** (anchor $115 — see `../BUSINESS/production-plan.md`)._

## Principle

The product sells on seriousness and permanence, not features. The object is the campaign. Marketing exists to place the object in front of the right people and get out of the way. No discounting, no decoration, no cheerleading — the same restraint as the page.

## Positioning statement

For men and women who hold themselves to standards, **On Record** is a permanent, finite instrument of accountability. It cannot be erased, refilled, or quietly abandoned. It is not a planner. It is not a journal. It is a record.

## Audience

Primary: leaders, operators, and disciplined practitioners who already value accountability instruments — founders, executives, military and veteran leaders, sales leaders, coaches.

Secondary (post-`v1.1`): couples, via The Common Record, reached through the individual editions' completers.

Anti-audience: anyone shopping for stationery, planners, or gifts by price. Do not chase them.

## Channels

| Channel | Role | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Seed partners (12–20 units from the seed run) | Credibility. Place units with named, respected holders. Collect written impressions at Day 30 and Day 90. | Unit cost only (production budget) |
| Founder-led LinkedIn | Primary organic reach into operators and leaders. 2–3 posts/week documenting the standard, the build, and the discipline — never "content marketing." | $0 |
| Website + waitlist (GHL, per `../WEBSITE/design-system/ghl-brand-kit.md`) | Single conversion point. One page, one action: join the waitlist, then preorder. | ~$100/mo platform |
| Product photography + one 60–90s film | The object must be seen to be believed. Dark, restrained, administrative — per `../BRAND/identity.md`. | $1,200–1,800 |
| Podcast guesting (leadership, performance, veteran, sales) | Borrowed audiences that match the register. Target 6–10 appearances pre-preorder. | $0 (time) |
| Email (waitlist nurture) | 4-email sequence: principle → object → seed-partner records → preorder open. | Included in GHL |

**Not permitted:** Amazon, marketplaces, discount codes, affiliate schemes, giveaway "delight" campaigns, mass paid social. Each contradicts the positioning (`../BRAND/positioning.md`).

## Sequence

1. **Now → Prototype (`v0.5`):** build the waitlist page; begin founder posts documenting the build; shoot nothing until the physical proof exists.
2. **Proof approved:** photograph and film the real object. Publish.
3. **Seed run (`v1.0`):** place 12–20 units with seed partners (`seed-partners.md`); sell the remainder to the waitlist at full price to validate willingness to pay.
4. **Day 30 / Day 90:** publish seed-partner impressions verbatim. No editing into testimonials — records, not reviews.
5. **Preorder (`v1.x`):** open a numbered, limited preorder (~250 units) to the waitlist first, public second. Full price. Close it when it sells out or the window ends — scarcity must be real.
6. **Production (`v2.0`):** convert preorder proof into the first production run. Introduce Woman on Record in parallel; The Common Record only after individual completions exist.

## Metrics

| Metric | Gate |
|---|---|
| Waitlist size at preorder open | ≥ 1,000 |
| Waitlist → preorder conversion | ≥ 10% |
| Seed units sold at full price | 100% of the non-placed seed run |
| Seed-partner Day-90 completion | ≥ 50% of placed units |
| Preorder sell-through (250 units) | ≥ 80% inside the window |

If the waitlist gate fails, extend the organic phase. Do not buy the audience.
