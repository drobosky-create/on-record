# The Journey to Market

_One page. Each step has a gate — do not advance until the gate is met. Detail lives in `marketing-plan.md` and `../BUSINESS/production-plan.md`._

## Twelve steps

| # | Step | Gate to advance | Tag |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Lock the pen spec and the InDesign file | Content final; pen chosen | `v0.5` |
| 2 | Qualify 1–2 binderies; order blank dummy, write test, foil draw-down | All three material tests pass | `v0.5` |
| 3 | Approve the full physical proof | Object communicates seriousness on open | `v0.5` |
| 4 | Open the waitlist page; begin founder posts documenting the build | Page live; posting cadence held | — |
| 5 | Photograph and film the proof | Assets match the brand register | — |
| 6 | Produce the ~50-unit seed run (~$55–87/kit) | Run received and inspected | `v1.0` |
| 7 | Place 12–20 units with named seed partners; sell the rest at full price | Sell-out at full price; no discounts | `v1.0` |
| 8 | Publish Day-30 and Day-90 seed impressions verbatim | ≥ 50% partner completion | `v1.0` |
| 9 | Open a numbered preorder (~250 units) at $95–125, waitlist first | Waitlist ≥ 1,000; conversion ≥ 10% | `v1.x` |
| 10 | Produce and ship the preorder batch (~$23–37/kit landed) | ≥ 80% sell-through funds the run | `v1.x` |
| 11 | Commit the first production run (~1,000 units, ~$13.50–22.50/kit) | Preorder proof; capital knowingly committed | `v2.0` |
| 12 | Introduce Woman on Record fully; pilot The Common Record with completers | Individual editions shipping reliably | `v2.0`+ |

## The shape of it

Prove the object (1–3) → build the audience while it's proven (4–5) → seed credibility (6–8) → let demand pull production (9–11) → extend the house (12).

Capital exposure is staged: ~$1.6K to hold a proof, ~$6K to hold the seed run, ~$15K to preorder (self-funding at ~85 preorders), production only after demand is on record.

The rule throughout: full price, real scarcity, no channel that contradicts the register. If a gate fails, hold the step — do not buy your way past it.
