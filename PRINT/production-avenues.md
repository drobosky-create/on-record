# Production Avenues

_Candidate vendors by stage, screened against `print-spec.md`. Every candidate still answers the five verbatim questions before receiving work. Status of record stays in `vendors.md`._

## Stage 0 — Hand-bound proofs (2–3 units)

| Vendor | Location | Why | Watch for |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Acme Bookbinding / HF Group** (First Edition + Harcourt Hand Bindery divisions) | Boston, MA | True hand/edition bindery serving publishers; **no minimums**; carries cloth stock in-house | Confirm silver foil on buckram; ask for the stock by name |
| Local fine-press / book-arts binder (via a guild directory) | Regional | Direct control, fast iteration on the dummy | Must handle 100–120 GSM uncoated text and true lay-flat sewing |

## Stage 1 — Seed run (~50 units, domestic short-run)

| Vendor | Location | Why | Watch for |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Bookmobile** | Minneapolis, MN | Strongest fit: hardcover runs **from 25 copies**, cloth case + foil stamps, serves publishers, labor-based pricing | Confirm sewn (not PUR-only) construction opens flat at ~140×210 mm |
| **Acme / HF Group** | Boston, MA | Continuity from proofs; edition binding with no minimums | Digital interior print quality on uncoated stock |
| **Digital City Printing** | New York, NY | Smyth-sewn cloth casebound at 25–50 copies; silver foil offered | Smaller shop — insist on dummy + full proof gates |
| **Bridgeport National Bindery** | Agawam, MA | 80-year bindery, hardcover in any quantity | Library/POD heritage; verify edition-quality cloth + foil finish |
| **Design Bindery (DESIGNBIND)** | USA | Layflat cloth notebook specialist; Euro-Rayon cloth, writing-grade text stock | Product is notebook-shaped — confirm it can build to our spec, not theirs |

## Stages 2–3 — Preorder (~250) and production (~1,000, premium offset)

| Vendor | Location | Why | Watch for |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Hemlock** (already under evaluation) | Vancouver, BC | Premium casebound portfolio incl. linen cloth + foil for brands and galleries | Confirm Smyth-sewn lay-flat and realistic minimums; likely the quality ceiling domestically |
| **QinPrinting** | Overseas (China), US sales | Smyth-sewn **by default**, cloth + foil, 100-copy minimum; strongest cost tier (~$9–15/unit at 1,000) | 8–14 week lead incl. freight; hard proof approval before sailing |
| **PrintNinja** | Overseas production, US service | Offset hardcover economics with US-based project management | Same lead-time discipline; verify uncoated 100–120 GSM options |
| **Mainland Printing** | Shenzhen, China | Smyth-sewn journal/lay-flat specialist; bulk pricing | Journal-market orientation — hold them to the print spec, not a planner template |
| **Bookmobile** (scale-up) | Minneapolis, MN | Produces to 3,000 copies — continuity from seed run without re-proofing | Per-unit will run above overseas offset; price the premium for domestic control |

Recommended posture: qualify **one domestic + one overseas** vendor at Stage 2 with identical proofs, then commit Stage 3 to whichever holds quality. Never switch vendors between preorder and production without a full re-proof.

## Packaging — rigid box

| Vendor | Why | Watch for |
|---|---|---|
| **PackHit** | No-minimum rigid boxes — fits the 50-unit seed run | Per-unit premium at low volume ($6–9) |
| **Packwire** | Custom rigid boxes, mid-volume DTC standard | Matte black + restrained silver only; refuse gloss upsells |
| **Taylor Box Company** (Warren, RI) | US heirloom-grade rigid setup boxes | Higher minimums; right partner at production volume |
| Book vendor consolidation (QinPrinting slipcase/box line) | One PO, one freight, guaranteed fit | Box quality must match the book — inspect at proof |

## Pen — permanent instrument

| Avenue | Fit | Watch for |
|---|---|---|
| Archival pigment ink pen, wholesale (e.g. Sakura Pigma via SNCO / Archival Methods) | Ink ethos is exact: pigment-based, fade-proof, chemically stable, permanent | Plastic body may read below the object; consider it the ink standard, not the final instrument |
| Premium metal pen, laser-engraved wordmark (custompens.com, DiscountMugs, or a named instrument such as a Fisher or Retro 51 body) | Object-grade feel; silver-on-dark engraving matches the cover system | Verify the refill is document-grade/archival, not standard ballpoint |

Decision required in `../PACKAGING/Pen/pen-spec.md` **before the Stage 0 write test** — the write test uses the shipped pen.

## RFQ package (send identical to every candidate)

1. `print-spec.md` verbatim — no commentary attached.
2. Quantities quoted separately: 50 / 250 / 1,000.
3. Required before award: named uncoated stock, blank bound dummy, foil draw-down on actual cloth, full physical proof.
4. The five screening questions from `print-spec.md`, answered in writing.
5. Reject on any of the listed reject conditions. Record all responses in `vendors.md`.
