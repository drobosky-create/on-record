# Paper Stock

## Interior
- 100–120 GSM uncoated
- Off-white or bone tone
- **Good ink holdout** — must be write-tested with the shipped pen (feathering, show-through, dry time)
- No pre-printed dot grid or ruling

## Candidate mills
- Mohawk (uncoated text weights) — evaluate off-white options
- _Add mill / stock names as binderies confirm what they carry._

The bindery must be able to **name the stock**. If they cannot, reject. Do not finalize paper until the write test passes on a bound dummy.
