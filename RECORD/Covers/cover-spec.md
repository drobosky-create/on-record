# Cover Specification

- **Wordmark:** the cover reads **"Man on Record"** — the brand / house name, not the product name ("The Record"). This is the only text on the cover.
- **Construction:** cloth (buckram) case preferred, or a heavy uncoated / duplexed board. **No matte film lamination** (it scuffs and fingerprints — wrong for an object about permanence).
- **Colour:** black or charcoal. No gloss, no imagery.
- **Finish:** **silver foil stamp** on the wordmark only, optionally combined with a blind emboss (registered foil-emboss). No subtitle, no other foil, no decorative deboss.
- Keep the wordmark lockup centered, restrained, and small rather than large.
- Prove the finish with a **foil/emboss draw-down on the actual cover material** before the run.
